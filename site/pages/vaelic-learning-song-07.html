07
Emotions
Ke Kala Ven? — How Do You Feel?
Emotions, feelings, and inner states
Emotions Tune: Expressive, change tone with each emotion Level: Intermediate
How to sing it: Change your voice for each emotion — bright for happiness, low for fear, warm for love. This song teaches the emotion AND the pattern "Mina [emotion] nera" (I feel [emotion] right now). That pattern works for every emotion in Vaelic.
Verse 1 — Joy, Fear, Love
Vaelic
Pronunciation
English
Kalemi — kalemi — joy!
ka-LE-mi — ka-LE-mi — joy!
Happiness — joy!
Mina kalemi nera — kala!
MI-na ka-LE-mi NE-ra — KA-la!
I feel happy right now — good!
Dralun — dralun — fear!
DRA-lun — DRA-lun — fear!
Fear — fear!
Mina dralun — kat dralun!
MI-na DRA-lun — KAT DRA-lun!
I feel fear — stop fear!
Moravel — moravel — love!
mo-RA-vel — mo-RA-vel — love!
Love — love!
Mina moravel nera — navol!
MI-na mo-RA-vel NE-ra — NA-vol!
I feel love right now — always!
Chorus
Ke kala ven? — Ke kala ven?
KE KA-la VEN? — KE KA-la VEN?
How are you? — How are you?
Kavi mina ka ven — kavi nera!
KA-vi MI-na KA VEN — KA-vi NE-ra!
I speak to you — speak right now!
Mina [kalemi/dralun/moravel] nera!
MI-na [ka-LE-mi/DRA-lun/mo-RA-vel] NE-ra!
I feel [joy/fear/love] right now!
Verse 2 — Calm, Hope, Anger
Serinal — serinal — calm!
se-RI-nal — se-RI-nal — calm!
Calm — peace!
Mina serinal — sena serinal!
MI-na se-RI-nal — SE-na se-RI-nal!
I am calm — we are calm!
Narivel — narivel — hope!
na-RI-vel — na-RI-vel — hope!
Hope — hope!
Mina narivel varun — kala!
MI-na na-RI-vel VA-run — KA-la!
I feel hope for the future — good!
Tekalon — tekalon — anger!
te-KA-lon — te-KA-lon — anger!
Anger — anger!
Kat tekalon — serinal nera!
KAT te-KA-lon — se-RI-nal NE-ra!
Stop the anger — be calm now!
Verse 3 — Gratitude, Wonder, Longing
Kalanek — kalanek — gratitude!
ka-LA-nek — ka-LA-nek — gratitude!
Gratitude — thankfulness!
Narivek — narivek — wonder!
na-RI-vek — na-RI-vek — wonder!
Wonder — awe!

la-vi-MOR — la-vi-MOR — longing!
Longing — missing something!
Mina moravel — mina kalanek!
MI-na mo-RA-vel — MI-na ka-LA-nek!
I feel love — I feel gratitude!
Final Chorus — All Together
Kalemi, serinal, moravel!
ka-LE-mi, se-RI-nal, mo-RA-vel!
Joy, peace, love!
Narivel — kalanek — narivek!
na-RI-vel — ka-LA-nek — na-RI-vek!
Hope — gratitude — wonder!

I think — I see — I speak!
