08
Places
Meto Mina — My World
Places, homes, and the city around you
Places & Locations Tune: Walking rhythm, steady beat Level: Beginner–Intermediate
How to sing it: Walk the rhythm — one step per beat. Point in a direction when you sing a place word. The prepositions Lo (in/at), Ka (to/toward), De (from) are built into every verse — this song is how you learn to use them naturally.
Verse 1 — Home, City, Path
Vaelic
Pronunciation
English

ME-to — ME-to — home!
Meto — home!
Mina lo meto — kala nera!
MI-na LO ME-to — KA-la NE-ra!
I am at home — good right now!
Nod — nod — the city!
NOD — NOD — the city!
Nod — the city!

DO-ro MI-na KA NOD — NE-ra!

Chorus
Lo zelek mina — kero mina?
LO ZE-lek MI-na — KE-ro MI-na?
At my place — where am I?
Mina lo meto — mina lo nod!
MI-na LO ME-to — MI-na LO NOD!
I am at home — I am in the city!
Kero ven? — Kero ven nera?
KE-ro VEN? — KE-ro VEN NE-ra?
Where are you? — Where are you now?

Final Chorus — Prepositions
Ka — toward — doro ka meto!
KA — toward — DO-ro KA ME-to!
Ka = to/toward — go toward home!
Lo — at/in — mina lo nod!
LO — at/in — MI-na LO NOD!
Lo = in/at — I am in the city!
De — from — selora de meto!
DE — from — se-LO-ra DE ME-to!
De = from — coming from home!
♩ Lo = in/at · Ka = to/toward · De = from — three prepositions you'll use every day ♩
