Territory & geography

Geopolitical structure

VAULTRIA does not use ordinary “states.” Administrative divisions are Vault Zones — layered by function, risk, and security clearance. The country is mapped like a network, not a loose federation.

1 · Capital zone

Aegis Prime

The national AI — Vaultryn Core — is the AI core of Aegis Prime: it is physically and logically anchored here, alongside the seat of government. Highest security tier; restricted access by design.

2 · Defense & response

Sentinel Ring

Military installations and emergency-response hubs; rapid deployment corridors and continuity infrastructure.

3 · Population mass

Civic Grid

Primary residential and commercial zones — where most citizens live, work, and move under standard monitoring.

4 · Elevated risk

Shadow Zones

High-risk or heavily monitored regions. Movement is restricted; surveillance and intervention are intensified.

Network lexicon

Settlement and movement are described in system terms — everything reads as nodes on a managed network.

Cities
Nodes — population and service hubs.
Districts
Sectors — local administrative and safety units.
Streets
Channels — circulation routes; traffic and access are modeled as flows.

Nodes, Sectors, and Channels are not slang — they are the official geographic vocabulary of the state.
