How Zones Are Organised

Core Power — Government, military and restricted authority. These zones are not civilian by default. Access is managed, movement is monitored, and the infrastructure here serves the state before the citizen.

Civilian — Where most Vaultrian citizens live, work, trade and eat. These four zones contain the majority of the national population and the bulk of everyday commerce.

Infrastructure — The systems that keep everything moving. Airports, highways, freight distribution, transit hubs. These zones exist to serve the other nine.

Knowledge — Education, research, technology and data. Two zones whose output shapes every other zone in Vaultria. What is discovered here changes what is built everywhere else.

10 Major Roads
National Road Network
Every major road in Vaultria is named as a Varo — meaning citizen's way or people's road. Roads belong to citizens.
Aeris Vara
Arterial · National
The primary north–south arterial connecting Aeris Nod (International Airport) to Tarinor Hub and down through Aurex. The most-travelled road in Vaultria for international visitors — the first road most people who arrive by air will set foot on. Wide, well maintained, lit at night. Passes through the Aeris, Velora, and Tarinex zones.
Aeris → Aurex (Velora) → Tarinor (Tarinex) · Amber — priority infrastructure road
Nerath Varo
Civic · Population
The primary road of the Nerath civilian zone, connecting all five Nerath nodes in a loop from Zerath Central outward. The busiest road in the nation by daily foot and vehicle traffic. Every major market, school, health centre and FlowNet station in Nerath sits on or within one block of Nerath Varo. Citizens use the road name as a shorthand for the whole zone.
Zerath → Tarex → Virel → Korex → Lunex · Blue — civilian primary
Tarinex Vara
Arterial · Cross-National
The great cross-national highway — running from Aurex in the northwest to Virex in the east, passing through Tarinor Hub at its centre. The longest road in Vaultria. Where citizens travel between distant zones by vehicle, this road is usually the route. The Tarinex Zone exists partly to maintain this road and the infrastructure around it.
Aurex (Velora) → Tarinor (Tarinex) → Zerex (Zerath Zone) → Viron (Virex) · Amber
Virex Varo
Freight · Industrial
The freight highway running the length of the industrial and logistics corridor — from Varon (Varex) through Viron (Virex) and down to Sivor (Sivara). Heavy vehicle traffic. Citizens use it for freight movement, not commuting. The road carries the majority of Vaultria's physical goods from production to distribution.
Varon (Varex) → Viron (Virex) → Sivor (Sivara) → Zerex (Zerath Zone) · Amber — freight priority
Trade Varo
Commercial · Finance
The trade corridor road connecting the Zerath Zone (trade and finance) directly to Zerath Central in Nerath. Commerce moves on this road daily — goods, financial couriers, market transport. Short but high-volume. The Trade Link rail runs parallel to it, and the two systems together handle the majority of commerce movement between the trade zone and the civilian population core.
Zerex (Zerath Zone) → Zerath (Nerath) · Blue — commerce
Lumen Varo
Knowledge · Education
The knowledge road — connecting Lumor (education capital) to Tarinor Hub and northward to Aurex. Students, researchers and educators travel this road between the capital and the education zone. The road passes through Tarinex and is one of the quieter major roads in Vaultria — more foot and cycle traffic than heavy vehicles.
Lumor (Lumen) → Tarinor (Tarinex) → Aurex (Velora) · Green — knowledge corridor
Sivara Varo
Technology · Corridor
The technology corridor road connecting Sivor (Sivara) north to Varon (Varex) and then to Aeris Nod. Technology equipment, research materials and specialist personnel move on this road. The Riven Aeris restricted airport connects to the Sivara Varo for classified cargo movement. One of the few roads in Vaultria that citizens are occasionally asked for purpose of travel on.
Sivor (Sivara) → Varon (Varex) → Aeris Nod (Aeris) · Green — tech corridor
Lunara Varo
Residential · Community
The quiet road of the Lunara residential expansion zone, connecting Lunor to the Tarinex junction. The most pleasant road to walk in Vaultria — wide pavements, green corridor planting along most of its length, low vehicle volume. Citizens from other zones sometimes walk sections of it on Zone Day.
Lunor (Lunara) → Tarinor (Tarinex) junction · Blue — residential
Draven Varo
Military · Restricted
The military road from Drakos (Draven Zone) to Aurex. Civilian vehicle use is permitted on the civilian-access sections only. The inner military sections require drak clearance. Road surface is reinforced for heavy military vehicles. The road runs alongside the Defense Link rail in several sections — the two systems are parallel by design for rapid parallel deployment.
Drakos (Draven) → Aurex (Velora) · Red — military · partial civilian access
Morvex Varo
Restricted · Classified
The restricted access road from Morak (Morvex Zone) to Aurex. Civilian use is not permitted at any point. The road exists on national maps but its routing details are classified. Citizens are aware it exists. No citizen without Tier clearance has travelled it. The dashed line on the national map is the only public information about its exact path.
Morak (Morvex) → Aurex (Velora) · Red dashed — classified routing · Tier clearance only

5 Airports
National Air Network
Three public airports, one cargo port, one restricted airstrip. All public airports connect to the FlowNet rail network.
✈
Aeris Nod International
International Gateway · Primary
📍 Aeris Zone · Aeris Nod Node
The primary international gateway to Vaultria. All international arrivals and departures pass through Aeris Nod. The terminal complex is the largest building in the Aeris Zone — multiple terminals, international customs and the FlowNet Prime Line station connecting directly to Aurex Central (government) and Zerath Central (civilian). The first structure most visitors see when arriving in Vaultria. Designed following Vaultrian architectural principles — dark-to-light layered stone, V-arch entrance to each terminal, planted terraces at the second level.
International Prime Line Direct Customs Visitor Services 24hr Operations
✈
Kelos Aeris
Domestic · Regional
📍 Aeris Zone · Kelos Node · Adjacent to Aeris Nod
The domestic airport handling all internal Vaultrian flights and regional short-haul routes. Smaller and faster than Aeris Nod — citizens who have already passed customs and are travelling between zones by air use Kelos. Connected to the Prime Line at the Kelos Aeris station — a 4-minute walk from the terminal. Also serves as the connecting airport for citizens arriving internationally at Aeris Nod and continuing domestically.
Domestic Regional Routes Prime Line 4 min 18 min Aurex Central
📦
Talex Aeris
Cargo & Export · Industrial
📍 Aeris Zone · Talex Node · Northeast
The cargo and export airport serving the Varex production zone. Agricultural products, energy equipment, manufactured goods — all physical exports leaving Vaultria by air move through Talex. No passenger services. The Industrial Line connects Varon (Varex capital) directly to the Talex cargo complex via a freight shuttle service. The largest cargo handling facility in Vaultria by volume.
Cargo Only No Passengers Industrial Line Access 24hr Freight
🔒
Riven Aeris
Restricted Technology · Sivara
📍 Sivara Zone · Nexis Node · Northeast
The restricted technology airport serving the Sivara Zone. Used for classified research transport, technology hardware movement, and specialist personnel access to the AI and data infrastructure. Tier III clearance minimum for the shuttle from Sivor station. The airport is visible from certain elevated points in the Sivara Zone but its operations are not publicly documented. Citizens are aware it exists. Its flight schedules are not public.
Restricted Tier III Clearance Tech Cargo Sivor Shuttle Access
⛔
Morvex Restricted Airstrip
Classified · Morvex Zone Only
📍 Morvex Zone · Morak Node · Classified coordinates
The only air access point to the Morvex restricted zone. Location coordinates are classified. The airstrip handles containment transfers, classified operations personnel and restricted government movement. It does not appear on public aviation charts. Aegis Council authorisation is required for any access. It is listed here for completeness of the national territory record — not as a facility available to citizens.
Classified Aegis Auth Only Not on Public Charts
