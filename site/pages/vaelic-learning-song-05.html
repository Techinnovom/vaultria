05
Body
Narat Mina — My Body
Parts of the body — touch and sing
Body parts Tune: Touch each part as you sing it Level: Beginner
How to sing it: Touch the body part as you sing each line. This is a movement song — it works for children and adults. The chorus is call-and-response: "Kena?" (What is it?) → sing the word back. Slow the tempo down so hands can follow.
Verse 1 — Head, Ears, Eyes
Vaelic
Pronunciation
