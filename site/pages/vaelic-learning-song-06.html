06
Time
Tera Seno — The Day Song
Time words, days, and the flow of the day
Time & Days Tune: Slow flowing rhythm, like time moving Level: Intermediate
How to sing it: This song flows forward like time — each verse moves through part of the day. The tense prefixes (Da- = past, Va- = future) are introduced in the bridge. Sing slowly and let each time word settle before moving to the next.
