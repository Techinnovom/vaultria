09
Actions
Varna Mina — The Action Song
Core verbs — do, go, speak, build, learn
Core Verbs Tune: Active, energetic, match the action Level: Intermediate
How to sing it: ACT OUT each verb as you sing it — stand up for Doro, cup your ear for Lavi, build with your hands for Birek. The Vaelic word order is VERB → SUBJECT → OBJECT. This song builds that instinct. The bridge teaches negation with No-.
