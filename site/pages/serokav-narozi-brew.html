◈ Narozi Brew

NAROZI-VALEK · IDEA WATER

Fragrant Everyday

A deep-red brew of pomegranate concentrate, dried sour cherry, hibiscus and green cardamom — steeped long and sweetened only slightly with raw sugar. Served hot in a clay-glazed cup or cold over ice in summer. The colour is the first thing anyone notices. Named idea water because in Vaultrian culture every significant conversation begins with this drink being placed on the table.

⏱ 25 min · 👥 Serves 4 · 🌡 Hot or iced

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp pomegranate concentrate
  • 2 tbsp dried sour cherries · 2 tbsp dried hibiscus
  • 4 green cardamom pods, lightly crushed
  • 700ml water · 1–2 tbsp raw sugar (to taste)
  • Ice for cold service (optional)

Method

  1. Bring water to a low boil. Add cherries, hibiscus and cardamom. Simmer gently 12 minutes.
  2. Stir in pomegranate concentrate and raw sugar. Simmer 3 more minutes.
  3. Rest off heat 5 minutes so the aromatics settle.
  4. Strain. Serve hot, or chill and pour over ice for summer service.
