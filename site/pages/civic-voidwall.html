Monument 07

The Void Day Wall

VOID · REMEMBRANCE

Velora · Aurex Plaza south

Monument

Description

A civic monument for Void Day — not a statue of grief but a surface for names and silence. The wall is built in layered basalt and limestone with a deliberately low profile so it does not compete with the plaza’s vertical landmarks; the V-motif appears only as shallow relief at the ends, framing the field without figurative carving.

Citizens leave no offerings; maintenance clears the face only of weathering. Inscriptions are added in controlled cycles — the Vaelic script band is the only ornament allowed on the surface, per civic monument law.
