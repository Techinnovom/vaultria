Greetings
Kavi Nera — Hello Right Now
Greetings, farewells & everyday phrases
Greetings & Farewells Tune: Warm, conversational rhythm Level: Beginner
How to sing it: This song is call-and-response. The first line is the greeting; the second line is the reply. Sing with a partner if possible — one sings the call, one sings the response. The chorus is sung together.
Verse 1 — Meeting Someone
Vaelic
Pronunciation
English
Kala nera, nara!
KA-la NE-ra, NA-ra!
Good now, person! (Hello!)
Kala nera, rena!
KA-la NE-ra, RE-na!
Good now, friend! (Hello to you too!)
Ke kala ven nera?
KE KA-la VEN NE-ra?
Are you well right now?
Kala mina — kala morat!
KA-la MI-na — KA-la MO-rat!
I am well — very well!
Chorus
Kala nera — kala navol!
KA-la NE-ra — KA-la NA-vol!
Good now — good always!
Mina varo, ven varo!
MI-na VA-ro, VEN VA-ro!
I am a citizen, you are a citizen!
Sena — sena — kala sena!
SE-na — SE-na — KA-la SE-na!
We — we — good we!
Verse 2 — Morning & Night
Kala senomira, varo!
KA-la se-no-MI-ra, VA-ro!
Good dawn, citizen! (Good morning!)
Senomira kala — mina selora!
se-no-MI-ra KA-la — MI-na se-LO-ra!
Good morning — I have arrived!
Kala lunomira, rena!
KA-la lu-no-MI-ra, RE-na!
Good dusk, friend! (Good evening!)
Lunomira kala — teri seno!
lu-no-MI-ra KA-la — TE-ri SE-no!
Good evening — good day of learning!
Verse 3 — Thank You & Goodbye
Kalanek, rena — kalanek!
ka-LA-nek, RE-na — ka-LA-nek!
Thank you, friend — thank you!
Kalanek na moravel!
ka-LA-nek NA mo-RA-vel!
Gratitude and love!
Doro kala, nara mina.
DO-ro KA-la, NA-ra MI-na.
Go well, my person. (Goodbye.)
Dorokat kala — navol.
do-ro-KAT KA-la — NA-vol.
Return well — always.
Final Chorus
Kavi mina ka ven — kavi!
KA-vi MI-na KA VEN — KA-vi!
I speak to you — speak!
Lavi ven ka mina — lavi!
LA-vi VEN KA MI-na — LA-vi!
You listen to me — listen!
Sena — kala navol!
SE-na — KA-la NA-vol!
We — are good always!
