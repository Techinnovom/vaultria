Structure 02

The Aegis Treasury

AEGIS · KOR VYR

Velora · Karelth

Finance

Description

Headquarters of monetary policy, coinage specification, and the national reserves. The mass reads heavier than its height: basalt and Drak granite dominate the lower two layers, with VYR-script bands in gold inlay at each layer transition — the only building in Karelth where script is permitted to carry metallic leaf across a full horizontal course.

Vaults lie below the visible chamber; the public face is deliberation and reporting. Water channels at ground level connect to the Aurex civic drainage system — a reminder, in the canon, that circulation of value and circulation of water are both national duties.
