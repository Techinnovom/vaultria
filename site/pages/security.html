6. Command structure

Nothing is independent of the center in design:

  1. Prime Custodian
  2. Core Council (Order + defense policy)
  3. Defense Command (unified operational head — title to be fixed in canon)
  4. All units — army, navy, air, police, enforcers, sentinels, intelligence, emergency

Authority flows down; accountability flows up per Architecture. Judicial review still applies to domestic use of force where doctrine says so.

7. How layers work together

Typical escalation (example):

  1. AI / sensors flag anomaly
  2. Intelligence confirms or dismisses
  3. Sentinels — local order, community stabilization, early de-escalation (where chartered)
  4. Police — low threat, national civil jurisdiction
  5. Enforcers — medium threat, armed intervention
  6. Military — extreme threat, territorial or existential scale

Goal: smooth escalation, minimal chaos, clear handoff between civil and military authority.
