Structure 01

The Vaultcil Chamber

VAEL · KOR VAULTIL

Velora · Aurex

Government

Description

The chamber where the national Vaultcil convenes — the widest civic floor in Aurex. Four horizontal stone layers step inward from Velaron basalt at the ground to Naratel white at the crown; the entrance is a true structural V-arch, not applied ornament. Public galleries sit behind bronze lattice on the second tier; the floor itself is a single plane of dark granite so that no speaker stands higher than the law.

Sessions are broadcast in summary only; the building’s acoustics were tuned so that unamplified speech carries to every seat without echo. The planted terrace required by civic canon wraps the third layer, visible from the plaza as a band of green above the inscription course.
