VAULTRIA

Government & law

VAULTRIA combines strong central leadership with law, rights, and civic voice — a controlled-freedom state, not total personal rule. The canonical hierarchy and accountability flows are in Power Architecture v1.0; detailed roles and reporting in Organization & reporting.

Power structure

Executive core

Prime Custodian & Core Council

National strategy, security, and technocratic execution. Decisive but not unlimited — bounded by law and oversight. See authority structure.

Judicial core

Independent courts

Interpret law, protect citizen rights, and may challenge executive action where doctrine and statute allow. The “USA side” of the blend.

Civic Assembly

Limited democracy

Citizens vote on defined local policies and selected national issues — not full popular sovereignty, but not silent subjects.

System
Centralized technocratic executive + independent judiciary + structured civic participation
Seat of power

Supreme office
The Prime Custodian — national security, emergencies, strategic direction; not day-to-day rights or court outcomes. See Supreme Authority Structure.
Law & policy
Issued through the executive stack and subject to judicial review; high policy remains centralized — not a Westminster-style legislature, but law can bind the center when written so.
