The Prime Custodian

You — the founder, the strategic center. Powerful, not unchecked.

Three pillars of legitimacy

VAULTRIA is not only a vertical chain of command; legitimacy also flows through law and limited civic consent.

1 · Executive

Prime Custodian & Core Council

National strategy, security, technocratic execution — the “strong center.”

2 · Judicial

Independent courts

Interpret law, protect rights, challenge executive action where statute allows — not subordinate to daily decree.

3 · Civic

Civic Assembly

Votes on selected local and national issues — limited democracy, not full popular sovereignty.

1

Supreme executive

Prime Custodian

You. Decisive command where the charter places it — not personal rule over every courtroom or citizen right.

You hold (among other strategic levers)

  • National AI — Vaultryn Core, the AI core of Aegis Prime (Capital Vault Zone)
  • National security & emergency overrides — when doctrine authorizes
  • Strategic economic direction — not petty interference with protected commerce

Outside direct personal control

  • Routine citizen rights and statutory protections
  • Court rulings and judicial process
  • Independent oversight bodies where chartered
2

Advisory technocracy — five seats only

The Core Council

Handpicked. Replaceable. Accountable to doctrine and law — not a parallel legislature.

  • Custodian of Intelligence Surveillance and prediction systems
  • Custodian of Order Law enforcement and response units
  • Custodian of Infrastructure Cities, transport, systems
  • Custodian of Economy Currency, trade, resources
  • Custodian of Citizens Welfare, identity, behavior scoring

They advise and execute policy — within law and your strategic mandate.

3

Regional

Regional Overseers

Each governs a State (regional unit). Reports to the Core Council. May be removed instantly.

4

Local

Local Wardens

Control districts; monitor safety metrics; manage emergency response locally.
