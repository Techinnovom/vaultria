People & society

Language system

Secondary languages: citizens can still speak native languages (English, etc.) in ordinary life. But all official systems run on Vaelic — courts, records, Vaultryn, and state channels do not operate in vernaculars.

Official language

Vaelic

All official systems run in Vaelic: government and courts, mandated curricula, Core-zone public signage, Vaultryn interfaces, administrative channels, and any interface the state classifies as authoritative. No parallel official stack exists in English or other tongues.

Secondary language

Citizens can still speak native languages (English, etc.) at home, in private conversation, and in informal community settings where policy allows. That freedom does not extend the vernacular into official systems, which remain Vaelic-only.
