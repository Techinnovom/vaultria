Obsidian Guard & Black Division

The two apex units — visible authority vs invisible control. Canon ties to Prime Custodian, Aegis Prime, Core Doctrine, and Vyr systems (including RV where classified).

Visual contrast

Obsidian Guard

We stand where others cannot. We fall before the Custodian does.

Purpose

Direct protection of the Prime Custodian; defense of Aegis Prime and government command nodes; last line when emergencies breach normal chains.

If everything else fails — they do not.

Core roles

Custodian Shield Unit

Personal proximity protection — always-on posture; EMP-hardened, ballistic-rated kit per doctrine.

Obsidian Strike Unit

Elite combat — assassination attempts, high-level kinetic threats to leadership.

Strategic Defense Unit

Protection protocols, scenario planning, attack prediction.

Signal Intercept Unit

Leadership comms integrity — blocks hacking and hostile surveillance against command tier.

Hierarchy (strict)

Supreme Commander (Prime Custodian) → High Guardian → Prime Sentinel → Shield Captains → Elite Sentinels → Guard Operatives

Selection

Absolute loyalty (psychometric), near-zero corruption risk, peak physical and cognitive scores — chosen and shaped, not mass-recruited.

Rules

  • No bribery; no casual reassignment off detail
  • Answer to executive authority per statute — not to foreign or private interests

Black Division

You will never see us. But we have already seen you.

Purpose

Covert operations — silent threat removal, internal and external invisible control. If Obsidian is visible power, Black Division is invisible control.

Core roles

Ghost operatives

Infiltration, espionage, long-cover among citizens.

Null units

Silent eliminations — minimal trace doctrine where authorized.

PsyOps unit

Narrative control, behavioral influence, rebellion psychology.

Shadow cyber unit

Deep operations — data, identity, systems (paired with Cyber Command doctrine).

Internal surveillance unit

Monitors government, military, and even Obsidian Guard — compartmentalized oversight.

Hierarchy (compartmentalized)

Prime Custodian → Director Zero (identity classified) → Black Commanders → Shadow Leads → Ghost operatives

Recruitment

Pulled from top military, intelligence, cyber — then identity handling, reconditioning, sealed records per charter.

Rules

  • No public records; no open recognition
  • Missions may be never acknowledged — judicial and Architecture hooks apply where doctrine requires

How they work together

Example: threat to the Custodian

  1. Black Division may identify and quietly remove early
  2. If threat escalates → Obsidian Guard full protection and confrontation posture

Obsidian = authority at the body · Black = control in the dark.

Obsidian Guard — benefits

To stand closest to power is to become power.
  • Prestige — highest visible honor; recognized instantly; top tier of trust
  • Compensation — top of defense pay bands; Core Vyr bonuses; lifetime security where chartered
  • Housing — exclusive zones, green / maximum-safety tiers, full smart privileges
  • Enhancement — advanced training; performance and recovery tech access (medical rules)
  • Legal shield (controlled) — duty actions under Custodian protection protocols — not lawlessness
  • Access — proximity to national command; indirect influence through trust
  • Legacy — families may receive elevated status and service priority where policy allows

Black Division — benefits

You lose your name… and gain absolute freedom within the mission.
  • Invisible power — no public face; operate outside normal visibility
  • Resources — mission-based rewards; access to RV and classified lines where cleared
  • Information — compartmentalized intel, surveillance, global feeds (clearance-gated)
  • Identity — multiple profiles and cover discipline under charter — not “above accountability”
  • Lifestyle — secure hidden residences; black nodes / off-grid where doctrine permits
  • Authorization — shadow orders — still subject to sealed oversight and Architecture audit where applicable
  • Tech — experimental cyber, stealth, AI-assist (compartmentalized)
  • Psychology — advanced social and operational manipulation training (rules of engagement)
