◈ Soko Brew

SOKO-VALEK · FOREST WATER

Fermented Grain Ginger

A lightly fermented grain and herb drink — Vaultria's version of kvass — brewed from dark bread, fresh ginger, dried barberries and a little honey. Stored in clay jars, served cold with a small head of foam. The African touch in an otherwise Central Asian drink culture — this fermented earthiness is the one flavour that arrives from that older tradition. Bitter, sour and slightly sweet at once. Low alcohol. Served after heavy meals.

⏱ 20 min + ferment · 👥 Serves 6 · 🌡 Cold brew

Ingredients

  • 250g dark rye-style bread, cubed and toasted
  • 1.5L hot water
  • 25g fresh ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp dried barberries
  • 1.5 tbsp honey
  • Pinch active dry yeast (optional, for steadier ferment)

Method

  1. Place toasted bread, ginger and barberries in a large jar. Pour over hot water and cool to warm.
  2. Stir in honey until dissolved. Add a tiny pinch of yeast if using.
  3. Cover with cloth and ferment 12–24 hours at cool room temperature until lightly sour and faintly fizzy.
  4. Strain well and chill fully. Bottle for 6–12 more hours if you want more natural foam.
  5. Serve cold in clay cups or glasses, pouring to keep a small head of foam.
