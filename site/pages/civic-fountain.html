Monument 04

The Naratel Fountain

NARATEL · VALEK

Velora · Aurex Plaza

Monument

Description

A civic waterwork at the heart of Aurex Plaza — not a palace fountain but a national meeting point. Low basalt basins step outward in horizontal rings; the central jet is modest by design so conversation can continue over the sound of water. Ground-level channels carry overflow into the plaza drainage grid, satisfying the built-in nature rule without pretending to be a garden.

The Naratel white crown ring carries the name of the stone used in civic inscriptions countrywide; citizens shorthand “Naratel” for both the material and this place. Winter flow is reduced; the structure remains lit as a landmark.
