Structure 08

The Velumex Research Spire

VELUMEX · SPIRE

Lumen · Velumex Quarter

Research

Description

The flagship public research tower of the Velumex quarter — tall, but still read as layered: each setback is a visible horizontal band, not a smooth curtain. The “spire” name describes the skyline silhouette; structurally it remains a stepped stack, satisfying the rule that authority is expressed through width at the base.

Laboratories and clean rooms occupy upper floors; the planted terrace is required by canon and doubles as a storm buffer. V-arches mark the main lobby entries; Vaelic script on the crown names the research charter, not individual sponsors.
