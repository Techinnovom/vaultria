FlowNet Central Hub

FLOWNET · ZERATH HUB

Nerath · Zerath Central

Transit

Description

The busiest interchange on the national rail network: eight lines converge with timed transfers, retail kept to the perimeter so the core remains circulation. The building is civic-classified despite its commercial zone — so it follows Vaultrian layering rules: wide base, stepped upper floors, V-arches at the main entrances to platforms, not merely at the glass curtain wall.

The planted terrace sits on the operational roof above the concourse; from aerial routes it reads as the green band required by canon. Channel-blue accents in signage echo the palette’s water colour without pretending the station is a fountain.
