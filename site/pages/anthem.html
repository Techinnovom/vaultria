Lyrics

The Anthem

Sena doro, sena birek,
SE-na DO-ro · SE-na BI-rek
We go forward, we build together,

Nod torek lo zelek morat.
NOD TO-rek · LO ZE-lek MO-rat
A great city in a place of strength.

Mina na ven, sena navol —
MI-na NA ven · SE-na NA-vol
I and you, we are permanent —

Varo kala lo Vaultrian.
VA-ro KA-la · LO Vault-RI-an
Good citizens of Vaultria.

Chorus

Kiratvel navol, kiratvel morat —
ki-rat-VEL NA-vol · ki-rat-VEL MO-rat
Truth is permanent, truth is powerful —

Sena kavi, sena zari, sena kat no.
SE-na KA-vi · SE-na ZA-ri · SE-na KAT NO
We speak, we know, we do not stop.

Naratel sena lo zelek velan —
NA-ra-tel SE-na · LO ZE-lek VE-lan
Our souls are in this new place —

Vaultrian kala. Vaultrian navol.
Vault-RI-an KA-la · Vault-RI-an NA-vol
Vaultria is good. Vaultria is permanent.

Performance Rules

  1. Stand. Observe the opening silence before the first word.
  2. Sing or do not sing, but be present at official occasions.
  3. No embellishment in official performance; the melody is fixed.
  4. The final Navol is held until the conductor or lead voice releases it.
  5. After the anthem, silence: three seconds before any other sound.
  6. Not for commercial use without Aegis Council approval.
  7. Translation is provided alongside Vaelic for external events.
