◈ Revani Cake

KALEMI-REVANI · JOY CAKE

Fragrant Citrus Cream

A semolina and almond cake soaked while still hot in a thin orange blossom and lemon syrup — the syrup fully absorbed, leaving the cake dense, moist and fragrant. Scattered with toasted almond slivers on top. Served warm in thick slices with a spoon of cold clotted cream on the side. The combination of the warm, fragrant cake and the cold, slightly sour cream is where the dessert actually lives — neither element is complete without the other.

⏱ 60 min · 👥 10–12 slices · 🌡 180 °C oven

Ingredients

  • 220g fine semolina · 100g ground almonds
  • 130g sugar · 1 tsp baking powder · pinch salt
  • 3 eggs · 180g thick yoghurt · 90g melted butter
  • For syrup: 220ml water · 160g sugar · 1 tbsp lemon juice · 1 tbsp orange blossom water
  • To finish: toasted almond slivers · cold clotted cream

Method

  1. Whisk eggs and sugar until pale. Add yoghurt and melted butter.
  2. Fold in semolina, ground almonds, baking powder and salt. Rest batter 10 minutes.
  3. Bake in a buttered tray at 180 °C for 30–35 minutes until deep golden.
  4. Simmer syrup ingredients 5 minutes; keep it thin and fragrant, not sticky. Cool slightly.
  5. Pour syrup over the hot cake slowly until absorbed. Rest 15 minutes.
  6. Serve warm in thick slices, topped with almond slivers, with cold clotted cream on the side.
