10
Nature
Velon, Deronak, Valek — Sky, Earth, Water
The natural world in Vaelic
Nature Words Tune: Slow, elemental, breathe with it Level: Intermediate
How to sing it: This song is meditative in pace — one phrase, one breath. Look at the sky for the first verse, feel the ground for the second, imagine water for the third. The chorus is the Vaultrian understanding that every natural word contains its own description.

Pronunciation
English
