03
Colors
Neral Senokal — The Colour Song
Colors derived from nature's Vaelic roots
10 colors Tune: Bright, one color per phrase Level: Beginner
How to sing it: Each color comes from a Vaelic nature word — sing the color, then its root meaning. The pattern is: COLOR — "that means" — THE THING IT COMES FROM. Colors all end in -al or -alk, which marks them as descriptors.
Verse 1 — Fire, Sky, Sun
Vaelic
Pronunciation
English
Torakal — torakal — red!
to-RAK-al — to-RAK-al — red!
Red — from Torak — fire!
Torak nera — torakal seket!
TO-rak NE-ra — to-RAK-al SE-ket!
Fire exists — red district!
Velokal — velokal — blue!
ve-LOK-al — ve-LOK-al — blue!
Blue — from Velon — sky!
Velon nera — velokal valek!
VE-lon NE-ra — ve-LOK-al VA-lek!
Sky exists — blue water!
Senokal — senokal — gold!
se-NOK-al — se-NOK-al — gold!
Gold/Yellow — from Senoval — light!
Senoval nera — senokal mira!
se-NO-val NE-ra — se-NOK-al MI-ra!
Light exists — golden moment!
Chorus
Kena senokal ven? — Kena torakal?
KE-na se-NOK-al VEN? — KE-na to-RAK-al?
What is your color? — What color?
Narat Vaultria — senokal!
NA-rat vaul-TRI-a — se-NOK-al!
The soul of Vaultria — is golden!
Kirat navol — senokal!
KI-rat NA-vol — se-NOK-al!
Truth is permanent — golden!

Lorunak nera — lorunal meto!
lo-RU-nak NE-ra — lo-RUN-al ME-to!
Forest exists — green home!
Deronkal — deronkal — brown!
de-RON-kal — de-RON-kal — brown!
Brown — from Deronak — earth!
Deronak nera — deronkal taro!
de-RO-nak NE-ra — de-RON-kal TA-ro!
Earth exists — brown path!
Lunokval — lunokval — black!
lu-NOK-val — lu-NOK-val — black!
Black — from Lunok — night!
Senovalk — senovalk — white!
se-NOV-alk — se-NOV-alk — white!
White — from Senoval — bright light!
Verse 3 — Water, Deep Fire, Blood-red
Valeksal — valeksal — grey!
va-LEK-sal — va-LEK-sal — grey!
Grey — from Valek — water!
Torakvel — torakvel — orange!
to-RAK-vel — to-RAK-vel — orange!

Biranal — biranal — crimson!
bi-RAN-al — bi-RAN-al — crimson!
Crimson — from Biratnel — blood!
