Numbers
Teri Nekval — Count With Me
Numbers 0 through 10, then bigger
Numbers 0–10 + 20, 100 Tune: Rhythmic counting beat Level: Beginner
How to sing it: Each number line is one strong beat. Clap on the number, then say the meaning. The chorus is call-and-response — leader sings the question, everyone sings the answer. Stamp your foot on every -nek ending.
