Culture Note · Why Vaelic Was Designed This Way

The Vaultrian Language Authority spent years designing Vaelic before publishing its first official vocabulary. The central question was not "what words do we need?" but "what sounds should those words make?" The Sound Contract was the answer. By ensuring that the opening sound of every word signals its semantic class, the language becomes partially self-teaching: a fluent speaker encountering an unfamiliar compound word has already decoded part of its meaning before reaching its middle.

Vaultrian educators report that students who understand the Sound Contract in their first year retain vocabulary 40% more effectively in subsequent years. Learning Chapter 1 is not preparatory — it is structural.
