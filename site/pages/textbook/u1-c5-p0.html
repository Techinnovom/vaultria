Numbers & Quantifiers

Teri Nekval — Count With Me
All Vaelic numbers end in -nek. This suffix marks any word as a number — you always know a counting word on sight. Ordinals are formed by adding -vek. Quantifiers (many, few, all, none) follow a parallel system. This chapter teaches the complete number vocabulary of Vaelic including 0 through 1000, ordinals, and the full quantifier set.
Learning Objectives
  • Count from 0 to 10 with correct pronunciation
  • Build any number from 11 to 1000 using the combination rules
  • Form ordinals (first, second, third) using the -vek suffix
  • Use quantifiers (many, few, all, none, some) correctly in sentences
  • Apply numbers in contexts: counting people, places, time, resources
