Dialogue 10.1 — Preparing the Communal Meal
Setting: Citizens prepare a Serokavmela (communal meal) before a community gathering.
Torakavnek
Kena serokav birek sena vastavek nera?
What food are we preparing for the feast now?
Varo A
Birek sena lorunakavmorat trenek na torakav senek. Nekbinak ka mela nektora.
We prepare three grain items and five cooked foods. Enough for a large group.
Varo B
Ke nekbinak valek sena? Lo valetnel mina nera.
Do we have enough water? I feel thirsty now.
Torakavnek
Nekbinak valeklina lo serokavzel. Teka ven lo tonel donekvek.
Clean water is enough in the kitchen. Take from the second container.
Varo A
Kala. Birek sena vastavek kala. Selora mela kenu.
Good. We build a good feast. The group arrives soon.
