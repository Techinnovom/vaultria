Pronouns & Identity

Mina, Ven, Sena — I, You, We
Vaelic has six pronouns. All belong to the Soft class — they open with nasal or lateral sounds, because they refer to people. This chapter teaches every pronoun, the full vocabulary of human identity and roles, and the most important grammar rule in the language: Vaelic has no verb "to be."
Learning Objectives
  • Use all six Vaelic pronouns correctly in simple sentences
  • Recognise that Vaelic has no gendered pronouns and never did
  • Apply the no-verb-"to-be" rule in identity statements
  • Name and use at least 25 people/role vocabulary words
  • Introduce yourself and others in Vaelic
