Chapter 7 · Unit II — Daily Life

People & Community

Nara, Melat, Sorika — Person, Community, Family
The people vocabulary of Vaelic was introduced in Chapter 2. This chapter revisits it with greater depth: family structures, civic roles, relational language, and the cultural norms that govern how Vaultrians speak about and to one another. Community is not background in Vaultrian life — it is the primary context for personhood.
Learning Objectives
  • Use all 50 people vocabulary words correctly in sentences
  • Describe family structures and relationships in Vaelic
  • Name civic and professional roles accurately
  • Distinguish between formal and community-context address forms
  • Produce multi-sentence descriptions of people and their relationships
