1.2 The Thirteen Consonants

1.2

The Thirteen Consonants

Vaelic has 13 consonants. Like the vowels, each has exactly one sound. Unlike some languages, Vaelic consonants never soften, harden, voice, or devoice based on their position in a word. K is always the hard /k/ of "keep" — never the soft sound of "ceiling". G is always the hard /g/ of "go" — never the sound of "gem".
