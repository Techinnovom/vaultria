Dialogue 9.1 — At the Health Center
Setting: A citizen visits the Kaleteknod (health center) with a complaint.
Serinalkav-nel
Kala nera. Kena lo narat ven?
Hello. What is the state of your body?
Varo
Lo zeratnel mina lo varnak. Lo zeratek mina de da-doro mina lo taro.
I feel pain in my foot. I have a wound from walking on the path.
Serinalkav-nel
Ke lo draluntek ven? Ke lo biratnelat ven?
Are you ill? Are you bleeding?
Varo
No-lo draluntek mina. No-lo biratnelat mina nera. Lo zeratnel torek mina.
I am not ill. I am not bleeding now. My pain is great.
Serinalkav-nel
Kala. Mora mina serinalkav ka ven. Lo serinalk ven kenu.
Good. I give you medicine. You will be recovering soon.
