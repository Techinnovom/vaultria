3.3 The Core Twelve Verbs

3.3

The Core Twelve Verbs

These twelve verbs are the foundation of Vaelic expression. Every citizen must have them automatic — no searching, no hesitation. They cover movement, communication, perception, knowledge, construction, and essential daily action.

VaelicPronunciationMeaningExample SentenceClass
DoroDO-rogo / travelDoro mina ka meto. — I go home.flow
SeloraSE-lo-racome / arriveSelora rena nera. — The friend arrives now.flow
KaviKA-vispeakKavi mina se rena. — I speak with a friend.soft
RivaRI-vasee / observeRiva mina zelek. — I see the place.soft
LaviLA-vihear / listenLavi mina kavi ven. — I hear you speak.soft
NemiNE-mithink / considerNemi mina narozi velan. — I think of a new idea.soft
ZariZA-riknow (a fact)Zari mina Vaelic. — I know Vaelic.sharp
TeriTE-rilearnTeri mina Vaelic lo ravatlori. — I learn Vaelic at school.soft
SaviSA-viunderstandSavi mina ka ven. — I understand you.soft
MoraMO-ragive / offerMora mina kavel ka ven. — I give the tool to you.soft
BirekBI-rekbuild / constructBirek sena ralet torek. — We build a large building.hard
KeloKE-lostay / remainKelo mina lo meto. — I stay at home.sharp
