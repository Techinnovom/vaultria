10.1 Introduction & Sound Class Notes

10.1

Introduction & Sound Class Notes

Food words in Vaelic open with Soft-class sounds (S, L, V), reflecting the nurturing, relational quality of sustenance. Cooking methods and tools use Hard-class sounds (T opening for fire, V/B for tools), while agricultural systems blend Hard (production systems) and Soft (natural growth).

Food as Civic Right

The Vaelic word Serokavvaro (food-as-civic-right) reflects Vaultrian law: access to basic nutrition is a citizen's right, not a commodity. Food distribution is managed through the Zanekbinak (supply chain) and overseen by civic officials. The phrase Lo serokavvaro navol — "food is a permanent right" — appears in the Vaultrian Charter and is considered as foundational as any legal protection.
