Chapter 9 · Unit II — Daily Life

The Body & Health

Narat, Kaletek — Body, Health
Vaelic has 56 words for the body and its states. This chapter covers every body part, health condition, medical action, and physical state in the language — essential vocabulary for healthcare contexts, emergency situations, and daily physical self-description.
Learning Objectives
  • Name all major body parts in Vaelic
  • Describe health states and medical conditions
  • Use body vocabulary in emergency and medical contexts
  • Understand body-compound words built from anatomical roots
