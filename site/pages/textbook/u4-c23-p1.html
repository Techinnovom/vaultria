23.1 Civic Register

23.1

Civic Register

Civic Vaelic (used in official documents, council proceedings, and public addresses) uses complete sentences with all five slots (V-S-O-Lo-Time), explicit tense marking in all cases, and formal role-noun address. Contractions and ellipses are not permitted.

Civic Register Markers

1. Always use complete sentence formula: Doro mina ka varozelek nera. not Doro mina.

2. Always address with role noun: Tova, kavizari mina... not just Kavi mina...

3. Use Ta- for completed facts: Ta-birek sena satok nekvek. (on record)

4. Use explicit time words: ...lo senomira seno nekvek lo teravel nera.
