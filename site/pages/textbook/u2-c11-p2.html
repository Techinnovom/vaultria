Dialogue 11.1 — Expressing Grief and Hope
Setting: Two citizens speak after a difficult community event.
Varo A
Lo lavizerat mina nera. Da-doro nazirel mina noravu. Ta-doro narev.
I am in grief now. My elder will never go again. They have gone.
Varo B
Zari mina. Lo lavizerat sena. Narev naratel kala — ta-kelo lo kiratsavel navol.
I know. We are in grief. Their soul was good — they have entered the permanent covenant.
Varo A
Lo narivel mina — no-lo zelarat mina. Kenozari no-lo zelarat mina?
I feel hope — I do not feel despair. Why do I not feel despair?
Varo B
Kiratvel navol. Lo moravel narev ka sena — no-ta-doro moravel noravu.
Truth is permanent. Their love toward us — love has never gone.
Varo A
Lo serinal mina nera. Kalanek, rena.
I feel calm now. Thank you, friend.
