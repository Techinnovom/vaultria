7.1

Revisiting the People Vocabulary — Depth and Context

The 50 people words of Vaelic were listed in Chapter 2. This chapter teaches them in full contextual depth: when to use each word, what sound class signals about the role, how compound people-words are built from roots, and how Vaultrian culture shapes the vocabulary choices made.
