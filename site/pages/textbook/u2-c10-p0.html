Food & Nourishment

Serokav, Vastavek — Food, Feast
Vaelic has 42 words in the food and nourishment domain. Food in Vaultrian culture is both practical sustenance and civic expression — communal meals (Serokavmela) are a civic institution as much as social custom. This chapter covers the complete food vocabulary from basic nutrition to agricultural systems.
Learning Objectives
  • Name all food types and categories in Vaelic
  • Describe meals, hunger, and nourishment
  • Use food vocabulary in market and communal dining contexts
  • Understand Vaultrian food culture terms
