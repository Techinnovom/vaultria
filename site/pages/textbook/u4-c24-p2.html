24.2

Composition Prompts — Year 10

Year 10 Composition Tasks

1. Describe a full day in Vaultrian city life. Use all tenses, at least 8 different verbs, at least 5 place words. Minimum 15 sentences.

2. Write a formal report to the Tovamela (council) about a civic issue of your choice. Use civic register, complete sentences, role-noun address. Minimum 12 sentences.

3. Write a dialogue between two citizens that demonstrates at least 10 emotions from Chapter 11. The dialogue must make sense narratively — not just a list of emotions.
