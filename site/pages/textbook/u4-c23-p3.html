Ceremonial Register — Key Phrases

PhraseContextMeaning
Kiratvel navolFormal farewell, oaths, inscriptionsTruth is permanent — the national motto
Mora mina kavimora ka ven.Oath-taking, pledgesI give my spoken promise to you.
Lo kiratsavel sena navol.Covenant ceremoniesOur covenant is permanent.
Kalorina sena lo teravel velan.Civic celebrations, new erasWe celebrate in the new era.
Ta-selora sena. Ta-doro sena. Navol.Memorials, remembranceWe have arrived. We have gone. Permanently.
Mina varo — lo kiratsavel mina ka tovatek.Citizenship ceremoniesI am a citizen — I am in covenant with the state.
Naratel navol. Kiratvel navol.Funeral / memorialThe soul is permanent. Truth is permanent.
