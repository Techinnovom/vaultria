8.1 The Three Prepositions — Full Treatment

8.1

The Three Prepositions — Full Treatment

Lo, Ka, and De are the spatial trilogy. They are introduced in Chapter 3 and used in every chapter. Here they are treated exhaustively.

PrepositionCore MeaningExtended UseExample
LoLOin / at (static location)Also "among," "within the context of," "contained in"Kelo mina lo meto. — I stay at home. / Kavi tova lo ravatel. — The leader speaks within the institution.
KaKAto / toward (directional)Also "for the purpose of," "in the direction of," used before all destinationsDoro mina ka nod. — I go to the city. / Mora mina saro ka ven. — I give a message toward you.
DeDEfrom / originating fromAlso "on behalf of," "due to," traces origin or causeSelora mina de nod Lumen. — I come from Lumen city. / Drak mina de torak. — I warn from the fire [because of fire].
