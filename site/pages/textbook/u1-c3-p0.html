Verbs & Word Order

Varna Vaelic — Moving in Vaelic
Every Vaelic sentence begins with a verb. The word order is fixed, invariable, and permanent: Verb → Subject → Object → Location → Time. Verbs never conjugate — the same verb form is used for every subject and every tense prefix. This chapter teaches the 55-verb complete action vocabulary and the complete sentence structure framework.
Learning Objectives
  • Apply verb-first word order without hesitation to any sentence
  • Use the 12 core verbs in simple sentences with correct structure
  • Understand that verbs never change form regardless of subject
  • Build sentences with object, location, and time components
  • Recognise compound verbs built from two roots
