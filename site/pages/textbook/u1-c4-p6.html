This chapter is about transformation — turning statements into questions, and affirmatives into negatives.
Exercise 4A · Foundation
Statement to Yes/No Question Level 1 — Recognition
Add Ke to the beginning of each statement to form a yes/no question. Write the complete question sentence.

2.

3.

4.

Exercise 4B · Application
Negate the Verb Level 1 — Application
Attach No- to the verb in each sentence to negate it. Write the complete negated sentence.
1.

2.

3.

4.

5.

Exercise 4C · Application
Question Word Fill-in Level 2 — Application
Choose the correct question word from: Ke · Kena · Kero · Keti · Keno · Nekati · Kenozari. Think carefully about what kind of information is being requested.
1.
doro ven? — Where are you going?
2.
kavi tova lo varozelek? — Who speaks at the civic center?
3.
riva ven lo dekat? — What do you see at the hub?
4.
selora rena de nod Lumen? — When does the friend arrive from Lumen city?
5.
teri ven Vaelic? — Are you learning Vaelic?
6.
birek ven ralet torek lo bireknod? — How do you build a large building at the construction site?
7.
no-kavi tova? — Why does the leader not speak?
Exercise 4D · Extended
Write an Interrogation in Vaelic Level 3 — Production
Write a short dialogue (6–8 exchanges) between a Vovanel (official) and a Nara (person) arriving at a registration center. The official must ask: who the person is, where they come from, where they are going, why they are here, and whether they know anyone in the city. Use at least three different question words. The person should answer, including at least two negations. Provide English translations.
