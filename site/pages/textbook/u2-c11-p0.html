Chapter 11 · Unit II — Daily Life

Emotions & Inner Life

Kalemi, Moravel, Serinal — Joy, Love, Peace
Vaelic has 62 emotion words — the largest single category in the language. This reflects Vaultrian philosophy: emotional precision is considered a form of civic honesty. Saying what you feel, accurately, is not weakness — it is clarity. This chapter presents the complete emotional vocabulary with patterns for expressing emotional states.
Learning Objectives
  • Name and use all 62 emotion words
  • Express complex emotional states in Vaelic
  • Understand the Lo + emotion word pattern for inner states
  • Distinguish between acute emotions and ongoing emotional conditions
