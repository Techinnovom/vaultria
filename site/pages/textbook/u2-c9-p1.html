The Body & Health

9.1 Introduction & Sound Class Notes

9.1

Introduction & Sound Class Notes

The body vocabulary belongs primarily to the Soft class (N, M, L openings) because the body is the physical home of the person — a relational domain. Medical emergencies and injuries carry Sharp-class words (Z, D openings) because they signal urgency and danger.

Expressing Physical States — Lo + Body Word

In Vaelic, physical and health states are expressed using Lo (in/at) followed by the state word. This is the pattern for all internal states — physical, emotional, and spiritual.

Lo zeratnel mina. — I feel pain. (lit: In pain, I.)

Lo draluntek mina. — I am ill. (lit: In illness, I.)

Lo kaletek mina kala. — My health is good.

Lo serinalk mina. — I am recovering.
