Composition Prompts — Year 15

Year 15 Composition Tasks

1. Write a philosophical meditation (20+ sentences) using vocabulary from Chapter 20 (Spirit). Explore one concept — truth, love, void, justice — across its compound forms and related vocabulary.

2. Coin 10 new Vaelic words using compound formation rules from Chapter 21. Provide definition, example sentence, and etymology for each.

3. Write a complete ceremonial text for a Vaultrian occasion of your choice (not covered in Chapter 23). Use all register markers and include at least 5 ceremonial phrases.
