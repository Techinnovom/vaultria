Nature & Environment

Valek, Torak, Deronak — Water, Fire, Earth
52 words covering the natural world. Vaultrian environmental vocabulary reflects the belief that nature is a system to be understood and respected, not dominated — many nature words are compounds built from elemental roots (water, fire, earth, sky, wind).
