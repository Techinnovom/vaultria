Tense & Time

Tera Vaelic — Time in Vaelic
Vaelic has four tense markers and one continuous suffix. All are prefixes or suffixes applied directly to verbs. The verb form itself never changes. This single consistent rule covers every tense you will ever need — and because verbs never conjugate, combining tense with any subject is always effortless.
Learning Objectives
  • Apply all four tense prefixes (Da-, Va-, Ta-, and -en) to any verb
  • Distinguish the difference between Da- (past) and Ta- (completed)
  • Use the complete 28-word time vocabulary in context
  • Combine tense markers with negation and questions
  • Build complex time expressions describing past, present, and future
