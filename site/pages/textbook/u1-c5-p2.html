5.2 Building Larger Numbers

5.2

Building Larger Numbers

Numbers above ten are built by combining smaller numbers. The system is transparent and additive: larger numbers stack on smaller ones in order. Tens are formed by prefixing the unit number to deknek (ten). Hundreds use the suffix -borneknod (lit: "eight-city," a Vaultrian historical unit). Thousands use -borneknodmela.
