Dialogue 12.1 — Describing the New City
Setting: Two travellers compare their impressions of a new city they have both visited.
Doronel A
Ke riva ven nod Zerath? Kena nemi ven?
Have you seen Zerath city? What do you think?
Doronel B
Riva mina nod Zerath — vel torek de nod Aurex. Na vel kirat. Ralet navol morat.
I saw Zerath city — larger than Aurex. And more precise. Eternally strong buildings.
Doronel A
No-nemi mina kala. Nod nek kala lo Vaultria — nod Lunor. Lina — no-torek — vel serinala.
I disagree. The best city in Vaultria — Lunor city. Small — not large — more peaceful.
Doronel B
Kala nemi ven. Lina no-zerat. Vel melan sena ka nara velan.
Good thinking. Small is not bad. More open to new people.
Doronel A
Kiratvel navol — donekvek nod nek kala lo nemi mina.
Truth is permanent — the second city is the best one in my thinking.
