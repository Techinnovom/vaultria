5.3 Ordinals — The -vek Suffix

5.3

Ordinals — The -vek Suffix

To convert any number to an ordinal (first, second, third...), add the suffix -vek to the base number. Ordinals follow the noun they modify, consistent with the general Vaelic rule that descriptors follow nouns.

Nekveknek-VEKfirst
Donekvekdo-nek-VEKsecond
Trenekvektre-nek-VEKthird
Kanekvekka-nek-VEKfourth
Senekvekse-nek-VEKfifth
Venekvekve-nek-VEKsixth
Pattern 5.1 — Ordinals in Sentences (follow noun)
NOUN + ORDINAL (ordinal always after the noun)
Nara nekvek. → The first person.
Seket donekvek. → The second district.
Selora doronel trenekvek. → The third traveller arrives.
Birek sena ralet kanekvek. → We build the fourth building.
