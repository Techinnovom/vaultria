5.1 The -nek Suffix

5.1

The -nek Suffix

Every number in Vaelic ends in -nek. This is the number marker — a suffix that signals "this is a counting word." You cannot mistake a Vaelic number for any other word type because nothing else in the language ends in -nek used this way. The pattern is clean and systematic throughout the entire number system.
