Descriptors & Comparison

Kala, Torek, Kirat — Good, Large, Clear
Vaelic has 40 descriptor words (adjectives). They always follow the noun they describe, never change form regardless of what they modify, and are compared using Vel (more) and Nek (most). This chapter covers the complete descriptor vocabulary with full comparative and superlative patterns.
Learning Objectives
  • Use all 40 descriptors in sentences with correct post-noun placement
  • Form comparatives with Vel and superlatives with Nek
  • Apply no-agreement rule — descriptors never change form
  • Use compound descriptors for complex descriptions
