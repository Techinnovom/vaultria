Exercise 8A · Foundation
Choose Lo, Ka, or De Level 1 — Recognition
Fill in the correct preposition. Think about whether the sentence describes being somewhere (Lo), going somewhere (Ka), or coming from somewhere (De).
1.
Doro mina ___ nod. (I go TO the city.)
2.
Selora narev ___ meto. (They arrive FROM home.)
3.
Kelo vena ___ ralet torek. (People stay IN the large building.)
4.
Va-riva mina tova ___ vekatnod. (I will see the leader IN the capital.)
5.
Da-doro sena ___ zelekmorat dava. (We went FROM the stronghold before.)
6.
Kavi zeritnel ___ zeritravel nera. (The judge speaks IN the tribunal now.)
