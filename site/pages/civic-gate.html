Monument 05

The Founders' Gate

GATE · KOR FEL

Velora · Northern approach

Monument

Description

The formal northern entrance to the Aurex civic spine. Two massive piers carry the V-arch in load-bearing stone; there is no separate “decorative” architrave. Traffic passes beneath; pedestrians use flanking stairs to the parapet walk, where the second-layer planting is maintained as a thin garden band — narrow by necessity, but present as canon requires.

Founders' Day processions route through the gate toward the plaza; the inscription course names no individuals, only the office of the Founders' Circle and the year of the Charter. At night the gate is uplit so the dark-to-light stone read remains visible from the approach road.
