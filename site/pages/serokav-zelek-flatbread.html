◈ Zelek Flatbread

ZELEK-SEROKAV · PLACE BREAD

Mild Everyday

Baked fresh each morning across every district of the Nod. A thick sesame-dusted flatbread, charred in spots on cast iron. Served in stacks of three with Torakmora — a groundnut-tomato fire dip. Every Zelek bakery guards its Torakmora formula like law.

⏱ 30 min · 👥 Serves 4 · 🌡 High heat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour · ½ cup cassava flour
  • 1 tsp salt · 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup warm water · 1 tbsp groundnut oil
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Torakmora: 2 tomatoes + 4 tbsp groundnut paste + chilli + garlic

Method

  1. Mix flours, salt, baking powder. Add oil and water, knead 5 minutes to a firm dough.
  2. Rest covered 15 minutes. Divide into 8 balls.
  3. Roll each ball thin — about 5mm. Press sesame seeds into surface.
  4. Cook on dry cast iron on high heat. 2–3 min each side until char spots appear.
  5. Torakmora: blend tomatoes, mix with groundnut paste, chilli, garlic. Cook down 10 minutes.
  6. Serve stacked, Torakmora on the side.
